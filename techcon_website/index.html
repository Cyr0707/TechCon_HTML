About TechCon: Our Story

Our Mission

TechCon was founded in 2018 with the core mission to serve as the **nexus of technological innovation**. We strive to create an accessible, inspiring, and collaborative environment where technology professionals, emerging leaders, and students can share groundbreaking ideas and build meaningful connections. Our ultimate goal is to accelerate the positive impact of technology on society.

Every year, we commit to fostering diversity and inclusion within the tech industry, ensuring that a multitude of voices are heard on topics ranging from ethical AI to sustainable hardware design.

Conference History

Beginning as a modest regional summit for software developers, TechCon has evolved dramatically. The first conference hosted 500 attendees focusing solely on open-source development. Today, we anticipate over 15,000 global participants.

The 2024 conference marks our seventh annual event, focused on the theme: **"Digital Renaissance: Rebuilding with AI and Sustainability."** This year, we emphasize cross-disciplinary learning and actionable insights for a greener, smarter future.
