Our Mission: Technology for Good

The primary mission of TechCon is to serve as the global catalyst for responsible technological advancement. We gather the brightest minds to discuss not only what technology can do, but what it should do, focusing on societal impact, ethical governance, and sustainability.

Our core principles include: Inclusion, Innovation, and Impact.

TechCon History: From Local Meetup to Global Stage

TechCon began humbly in 2018 as a small, regional developer meetup focused on open-source contributions. Held in a university auditorium, the first event hosted just 300 participants.

By 2020, we pivoted to a hybrid format and expanded our reach globally, attracting thousands of virtual attendees and securing our first major keynote speaker from Silicon Valley. Today, TechCon is recognized as a mandatory stop on the international tech conference circuit.

Notable Past Speakers

We are proud to have hosted some of the most influential figures in the technology landscape:

Dr. Anya Sharma (2021)

Contribution: Dr. Sharma, a pioneer in decentralized finance, delivered a seminal talk on the future of digital currency and global accessibility. Her session, "The Uncensorable Ledger," remains one of our most viewed recordings.

Kenji Tanaka (2019)

Contribution: Kenji Tanaka, the lead engineer behind Project Chimera Robotics, shared insights into the ethical development and physical limitations of human-scale bipedal robots. His demonstration was a major highlight of the 2019 conference.
